NDT - Metals and Alloys

Non-Destructive Testing (NDT) is crucial for evaluating the integrity of metals and alloys without causing damage. At OIAL, we offer a comprehensive range of NDT services to ensure the quality and safety of your materials, helping you meet industry standards and specifications.

Our Capabilities

  • Visual Inspection
  • Liquid Penetrant inspection
  • Magnetic Particle inspection
  • Ultrasonic inspection
  • Ultrasonic Thickness Gauge
  • Radiography
  • Portable Hardness Test

We provide precise and reliable chemical analysis for a wide range of metals and alloys, ensuring quality and compliance for your materials.

Metallography Testing

Metallography testing is essential for evaluating the microstructure and properties of materials, ensuring they meet industry standards and performance requirements. At OIAL, we offer a comprehensive range of metallography testing services to assess the structure, grain size, and other critical characteristics of metals and alloys.

Our Capabilities

  • Microstructure, Macrostructure
  • Grain Size, Grain Flow
  • Effective case depth
  • Total Case Depth
  • Depth of Decarburization
  • Non Metallic Inclusion Content
  • Depth of surface defect
  • Graphite Type & Distribution in Cast Iron
  • Segregation of Carbides
  • Depth of Cladding
  • Porosity Content
  • Coating Thickness
  • Weld Penetration Measurement
  • Jominy End Quench Test
  • Coating / Plating Thickness
  • Ferrite Content
  • Failure Analysis, etc.

We provide precise and reliable chemical analysis for a wide range of metals and alloys, ensuring quality and compliance for your materials.
